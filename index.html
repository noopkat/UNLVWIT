Grace Hopper = badass

- Popularised the term "debugging"

- Led the team who defined the COBOL language

- In 1952 Hopper had an operational compiler. "I had a running compiler and nobody would touch it. They told me computers could only do arithmetic." - Hopper

Just because you don't see your gender represented, doesn't mean that you're not good enough or smart enough to get there.

You belong where your passion is, no questions asked

How do you stay happy and successful in potentially sexist and toxic environments?

Call out the BS when you're feeling it

Encourage critical thinking from colleagues and classmates

Attend related meetups that support you

Extend your network and find some role models and mentors

Know that you're not alone

Working in tech rocks, let's keep it that way and make it better for everyone
